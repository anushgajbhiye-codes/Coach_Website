Questions

Everything you
need to know

How is online coaching different from a gym program? +
Online coaching with Sonu is fully personalised and adaptive. Unlike static gym programs, every week your plan evolves based on check-in data, how you're responding, life events, and more. You get the expertise of a top-tier coach without being geographically limited.
Do I need to be experienced to start? +
Not at all. Sonu coaches everyone from total beginners to competitive athletes. There is a dedicated Beginner Program for those new to training, with extra form guidance and video demonstrations for every exercise.
How does the check-in process work? +
You'll fill in a weekly form covering your weight, energy, sleep, adherence, and any issues. You can attach photos for body composition tracking. Sonu reviews every check-in personally and sends back updated notes or program changes within 24 hours.
What equipment do I need? +
Sonu programs for your situation — full gym, home gym, or minimal equipment. You'll be asked about available equipment in your onboarding form, and your program will be built accordingly.
Is nutrition included in all plans? +
Yes. Every plan includes macronutrient targets, food timing guidelines, and a flexible food list. Higher tiers include more detailed meal structure and supplement guidance. No plan uses a generic 1200-calorie template.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. All plans are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel with 7 days' notice before renewal. If you need to pause due to life circumstances, that option is also available.
